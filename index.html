Features

Harness the power of DEXes to purchase Solana tokens faster than you can say guacamole. Our Telegram bot ensures fast, easy and secure transactions, anytime, anywhere!

Instant Buys

Simply input a token address into the bot, and a purchase transaction will immediately execute using the preset amount of SOL in your settings.

Limit Buys

Choose which coin you would like to buy, along with the price you are willing to purchase at. We'll take care of the rest.

Auto DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) (Coming Soon)

AvocadoBot allows users to scale into a coin over time. Set it once and relax: our service will continually purchase the coin for you, using your specified amount of SOL, over the time you set.

Token Deep Analysis

Our AI tool is built to provide you with a deep analysis about any token. The analysis will immediately provide information such as LP burn percentage, mint authority, freeze authority, and top 10 holder concentration to ensure you have the information you need when purchasing tokens.

Social Activity Analysis (Coming Soon)

Get the scoop on who is chatting about your favorite coins.
